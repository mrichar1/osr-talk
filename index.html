## Beekeeping and Oilseed Rape ![](img/osr.jpg) #### Matthew Richardson - 9 December 2019
## OSR: A History Notes: * OSR grown for thousands of years in far-east * Oil predominantly used for illumination and fuel from the 13th century * Took off with industrial revolution for soap, lubricants * Significant food use from 1960s with move from butter to margarine * Erucic acid discovered - heart attacks! * Fixed with Canola * Byproducts no good for animal feed due to glucosinolates * Glucosinolate-free strains now exist. * Third largest oil source after Soybean and Palm, 2nd largest animal feed after Soybean
